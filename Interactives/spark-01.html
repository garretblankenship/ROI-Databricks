Welcome to the cluster

Apache Spark is a distributed computing engine: it spreads one big job across many machines so they can work on it together.

Two kinds of processes do that work. Use the tabs above to explore each scenario, or click the nodes in the diagram to learn what each one does.

Key Concept Spark splits work across a cluster. One driver coordinates; many executors do the actual computation in parallel.
