SCENARIO · 01
Tasks fill slots

An executor isn't one worker — it's a JVM process with multiple slots, each tied to a CPU core. One slot runs one task at a time.

Total parallelism = sum of slots across all executors. If you have 12 tasks and 6 slots, you'll need 2 waves of work.

Key Concept Partitions → tasks → slots. Wall-clock time ≈ (tasks ÷ slots) × time-per-task.
